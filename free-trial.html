COMMON QUESTIONS

Do I need any experience?

No. Our trial class is designed for complete beginners. You'll be guided every step of the way.

What should I wear?

Comfortable athletic clothing (shorts/leggings and a t-shirt). We have loaner gis available for your first class.

Will I have to spar?

No. Sparring is optional until you're comfortable. Your first class focuses on learning technique with a partner.

Can my child try a class too?

Yes. Kids ages 7-12 can attend a free trial class. Kids ages 7-12 are welcome — learn more about our kids program.
