HOW WE TRAIN

ECOLOGICAL, CONSTRAINT-LED APPROACH

Our kids program uses modern coaching methods that develop real problem-solving ability — not just memorized moves.

Each class includes:

  • → Constraint-led games that require thinking, not memorizing
  • → Safe, controlled positional situations that teach technique
  • → Partner tasks that build communication & leadership
  • → Concept-based lessons that apply across ALL sports
  • → Dedicated assistant instructors for individual attention

YOUR CHILD'S FIRST CLASS

  • Children are welcomed and paired with helpful partners while parents watch from the seating area.
  • Fun warm-up games build agility, balance, and coordination.
  • Learn one simple, safe movement focused on control and awareness.
  • Drill through structured reps guided by the coach.
  • Play a jiu jitsu related game to apply what they learned.
  • Every child stays actively engaged, building responsibility, problem-solving, and resilience.
