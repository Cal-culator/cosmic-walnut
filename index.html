WHAT TO EXPECT

ADULTS' FIRST CLASS

  • Arrive a few minutes early and meet the coach.
  • Get paired with a friendly training partner ("mat buddy").
  • Warm up with simple movements and drills.
  • Learn one foundational technique (sweep, guard retention, takedown, etc.).
  • Drill with partners under close supervision.
  • Optional light positional training if you feel comfortable.
  • Ask questions anytime — beginners are fully supported.

KIDS' FIRST CLASS

  • Children are welcomed and paired with helpful partners while parents watch from the seating area.
  • Fun warm-up games build agility, balance, and coordination.
  • Learn one simple, safe movement focused on control and awareness.
  • Drill through structured reps guided by the coach.
  • Play a jiu jitsu related game to apply what they learned.
  • Every child stays actively engaged, building responsibility, problem-solving, and resilience.

FAQ

Do I need prior experience? Do I need a Gi?

No experience required. Every class is beginner-friendly and step-by-step. New students can borrow a loaner Gi if needed.

Do I have to roll (spar) on my first day?

No. Rolling is optional until you're ready. We gradually introduce beginners to live training at a comfortable pace.

How do trial classes work?

Sign up, show up, and try a session for free. You'll meet the coach, get paired with safe partners, and learn how the program works.

What about safety?

Safety is our top priority. We pair beginners with trustworthy, experienced partners and emphasize controlled, technical training — not ego.
