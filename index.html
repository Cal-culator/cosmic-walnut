CLASSES
OFFERED

01 OPEN TO ALL LEVELS

BEGINNERS

BUILT FOR YOUR FIRST DAY ON THE MAT

No experience needed. This class breaks down the fundamentals step by step, so you're learning real technique from day one without getting thrown into the deep end.

What to expect:

  • → Clear, one-concept-at-a-time instruction
  • → Guided drilling with a partner, at your pace
  • → A coach who checks in, not just calls out
  • → Optional light positional rolling (sparring) once you're ready
  • → No pressure to spar until you want to
02 OPEN TO ALL LEVELS

ADVANCED

FOR THE STUDENT READY TO ROLL HARDER

Once the basics are second nature, this is where technique gets tested. Faster pace, harder rolls, and details that separate a good roll from a great one.

What to expect:

  • → Higher-intensity rounds with experienced training partners
  • → Advanced positional concepts and chain-linked techniques
  • → Constraint-led sparring to sharpen specific weaknesses
  • → Deeper technical breakdowns as concepts get more complex
  • → Video review + match analysis (regularly integrated)
03 OPEN TO ALL LEVELS

COMPETITORS

PREPARE TO COMPETE

Open to any level. Whether you're prepping for your first tournament or your fifteenth, this class is built around one goal: winning.

What to expect:

  • → Competition-specific game planning and strategy
  • → Live, hard pace rounds that mirror tournament pressure
  • → Situational sparring from common competition positions
  • → Weight, timing, and match-management guidance
  • → Coached by a multi-time gold medalist who's been exactly where you're headed

FAQ

Do I need prior experience? Do I need a Gi?

No experience required. Every class is beginner-friendly and step-by-step. New students can borrow a loaner Gi if needed.

Do I have to roll (spar) on my first day?

No. Rolling is optional until you're ready. We gradually introduce beginners to live training at a comfortable pace.

How do trial classes work?

Sign up, show up, and try a session for free. You'll meet the coach, get paired with safe partners, and learn how the program works.

What about safety?

Safety is our top priority. We pair beginners with trustworthy, experienced partners and emphasize controlled, technical training — not ego.
